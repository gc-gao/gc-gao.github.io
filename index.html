About Me

I am a second year Computer Science PhD student at the Department of Computer Science at the Peking University. My supervisor is Shaofeng Jiang . I obtained my master's degree under the supervision of Yong Zhang from SIAT , and my bachelor's degree from Northeast Forestry University .


My current research direction is algorithms for massive data sets, particularly geometric problems in the MPC models.

The following topics are of academic interest to me:

  • Distributed and parallel algorithms
  • Approximation Algorithms, Online Algorithms
  • Design and Analysis of Algorithms in Computational Economy
